Health (midgame)
Buy health. Keep buying health. No matter how expensive the cash and coin cost gets, buy all the health. Don't forget the health lab research.

Thorns, should be topped off.
You should have orb speed maxed, as well.
Shockwave to push enemies into orbs.
Equip Inner Orbs and Death Ray if you have it.
