Medals
Medals are currency used to make purchases from the Event Shop.
Obtaining Medals
Medals can be obtained from three sources:
Completing
Event
Missions
Earning Weekly Rewards from
Daily Missions
Guardian
with Fetch module equipped
Spending Medals
Medals can only be spent in the Event Shop and do not expire when an Event ends. Players can save their Medals for a future Event Shop if they choose to do so. Items offered in the Event Shop include the following:
Gems
Power Stones
Shards
Themes
Music
Bots
Relics
(Only during an Event rerun)
Relics
Relics
can only be unlocked by earning Medals during their respective
Event
; any Medals carried over from a previous Event or earned from
Daily Mission
boxes are not counted toward the number of Medals needed to earn a Relic.
