Description
Damage each projectile deals to enemies. Damage is the base state for calculating both tower projectiles and other abilities such as land mines and Ultimate Weapons.
Workshop
Unlocked in Workshop at the start of the game. Maximum of 6000 levels in the workshop. Damage can be increased using cards, relics, Modules and Workshop Enhancements.
Damage Formula
To get the value used in game, avoid any rounding.
Displayed damage = Workshop x Lab x Damage Card x (1 + Relics) x (1 + Cannon Module %) x Enhancements x Perk
1
+ Berserker
2
Perk = (1 + 0.15 x Quantity) x (1 + Standard Perk Bonus), Do not round answer.
Berserker maxes at +700% damage factoring all other bonuses in the formula.

Cards
There are 3
cards
that modify damage: Damage, Berserker and Super Tower. Card multipliers will be multiplied with other multipliers including other cards. Damage and Berserker will change the displayed workshop value. Super Tower modifies bullet damage on hit and does not change the workshop value. This also means Damage and Berserker increase
Ultimate Weapon
damage and Super Tower does not. Card masteries introduce other ways to boost tower damage, and other cards such as UW Crit and Crit Chance can increase Ultimate Weapon damage.
Damage
Super tower
Berserker
Damage
The Damage card is a common card that multiplies workshop damage by the card multiplier. The card has an increased % to be obtained as the first card you buy.
Super Tower
Super tower is an epic card that temporarily increases Tower damage. The damage from Super Tower does not increase Ultimate Weapons or any ability that factors tower damage. Only tower projectiles damage is increased by the card. The card is a locked card that cannot be removed during a round
Berserker
The Berserker card is an epic card that does not act as a multiplier. The card "absorbs" all of the damage you take. The card is not reducing your damage received but instead stores the total damage without reductions and multiplies the total received by the % based on card level. The % of damage is then added to your total damage with a limit of +700% or 8x base. Since it is added, increasing base damage can cause Berserker to no longer be maxed.
Ex: damage taken 1,000,000, berserker 1%, tower damage after multipliers 1,000
1,000,000×0.01 = 10,000 bonus damage stored
1,000 x 7 = 7,000 limit
10,000>7,000 so 7000 damage added, 3,000 unused.
If damage is later increased to 2,000 w/o berserker, then limit = 2000 × 7 = 14,000. All bonus damage is used if no other damage was taken.
