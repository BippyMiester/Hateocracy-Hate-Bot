Description
Attack speed is the number of projectiles fired from your tower. Attack speed also increases the travel speed of a projectile from your tower to an
enemy
(including Energy Net and Plasma Cannon).
F
or very low attack speeds the attack speed value is equivalent to projectiles fired per second (before factoring in rapid fire, multishot, and bounce shot). For medium attack speeds the attack speed value is closer to bullets fired per 2 seconds and for very high attack speeds (over 40) the amount contributed per point in attack speed is further diminished.
Attack speed has had performance changes in V27 and is still under analysis. Game performance should allow for more bullet per second to be accurately calculated.
Once the '
Light Speed Shots
' lab research has been completed, the projectiles will travel at the maximum movement speed possible, and therefor their travel speed is no longer affected by Attack Speed (this does not affect Energy Net or Plasma Cannon).
Workshop
Unlocked in the
workshop
for free. Attack speed has 99 levels in the workshop with a base of 1.00 where each level increases by 0.05 for a total of 5.95.
Formula
To get the value used in game, avoid any rounding.
Displayed Attack Speed = (Workshop x Lab x Card + Module Sub Effect) x Enhancement
