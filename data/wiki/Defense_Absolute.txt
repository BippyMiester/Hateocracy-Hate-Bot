Card
The Fortress
card
increases the workshop value of defense absolute. The card is multiplied with the lab.
Calculation
Defense absolute is calculated after def % is applied. See example below:
Damage - 100, Def % - 25%, def abs - 50
Damage received = 100 * (1 - 0.25) - 50 = 25
* Enemies have a heat-up ability: for each hit, their damage is increased x1.04. The damage displayed may not be the damage they are dealing if you are being hit often.
Workshop Enhancement
Defense Absolute
Requires 500 billion coins to be spent on defense enhancements to unlock. Increases the Defense Absolute multiplier by x1 when unlocked and increases by 0.01x per level. Has a maximum level of 400 for a 5x bonus.
