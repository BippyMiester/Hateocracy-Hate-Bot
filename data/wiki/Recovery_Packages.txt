Overview
Recovery Packages have a chance to spawn and restore tower health by a % up to a maximum % of tower health.  Packages are automatically applied upon spawning -- they do not need to be manually collected.
Workshop
Unlocks in
workshop
for 1,500,000 coins after
Interest
. Unlocks Recovery Amount, Max Recovery and Package Chance.
Recovery Amount
Amount of bonus
health
each packages adds to your tower. Each package grants an additional amount based on this percentage of your tower's current maximum health. Has 300 levels with a base of 14%. Each level increases health by 0.40% for a maximum of 134.00%.
Max Recovery
How much beyond max health recovery packages are able to recover. Has 500 levels with a base of x1.00 health. Each level adds 0.031 to the max health for a maximum of x16.50 health.
Packages can restore beyond your maximum health.
Lifesteal
and
health regen
are unable to heal above 100% health.
Package Chance
Chance a recovery package can spawn each wave. Has 60 levels with a base value of 6%. Each level increases the chance to spawn by 0.40% per level for a maximum of 30%.  Package Chance has a max of 82% when both the lab(4%) and card(33%) are maxed in addition to an Ancestral Substat(15%).
Card
The Recovery Package Chance
card
increases the package chance workshop.
