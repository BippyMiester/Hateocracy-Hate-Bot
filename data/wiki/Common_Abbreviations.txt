Ultimate Weapons (UW)
BH - Black Hole
CF - Chrono Field
CL - Chain Lightning
DW - Death Wave
GT - Golden Tower
ILM - Inner Land Mines
PS - Poison Swamp
SL - Spotlight
SM -  Smart Missiles
Adding a "+" to any of these usually refers to the Ultimate Weapon Plus(UW+) abilities unlocked for stone purchase after unlocking all 9 UWs. The number following usually refers to the level of that UW+, starting at 0 at base unlock.
Additionally, sometimes a letter(s) will be added onto a UW to notate a stat of that specific ultimate weapon such as the following suffixes:
d - Damage
dur - Duration
CD - CoolDown
b - Bonus
q - Quantity
c - Chance
A - Angle
SR - Speed Reduction

Adding a "#" or "m" to any of these Usually refers to that cards Mastery, which is unlocked for purchase with stones after T16W100 and all cards are maxed. The number following usually refers to the labs level of that card mastery, starting at 0 at base unlock.

Common Lazy Talk
BC - Battle Condition
BW - Bounce Web, a recent strategy that involves maximizing projectile counts to trigger Pulsar Harvester more frequently.
CB - Coin Bonus
CC - Crowd Control (anything that hinders enemies from hitting you)
CPM - Coins per Minute
CPH - Coins per Hour
Devo - Devolve, encompasses strategies that devolve killing power to a specific time or place for coin benefit
DWEW - Death Wave Effective Wave
eHP - effective Health Points. How much damage you can survive, after taking any damage mitigation into account
ePaths - Effective Paths Collection. A set of Google sheets to help calculate specific ROI of upgrades per player.
F2P - Free to Play
GC - Glass Cannon
LTC - Life Time Coins
LTS - Life Time Stones
P2P - Pay to Play
pb - Personal Best
PWR - Perk Wave Requirement
RNG - random number generator, refers to random chance
ROI - Return on Investment
SMEW - Smart Missile Effective Wave, wave where SM volley stops killing in SMax Devo
SPB - Standard Perk Bonus a Perk lab
T(D)C - Tower (Data) Collection, a huge compilation of incredibly useful optimisation tools by Cris and community
WAWSIS - Wave Accelerator + Wave Skip + Intro Sprint. This used to be a tournament tactic based on running out the clock with high card levels, but is now more commonly a high tier farming tactic using the WAWSIS card masteries.
