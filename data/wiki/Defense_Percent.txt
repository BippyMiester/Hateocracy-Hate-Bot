Card
The Extra Defense card adds to the base workshop amount. Using the defense % lab, the Extra Def
card
and Defense %
perk
, defense percent can reach 95.5% however, there is a limit of 98%.
Other sources of damage reduction such as
Chrono Field Damage Reduction
Primordial Collapse
, and
Flame Bot
are separate from Defense % and do not count toward the 98% limit, but performs their damage reduction calculations after Defense Absolute instead.
Lab
Defense Percent
The
lab
is unlocked in
Milestones
on tier 5 wave 30. There are 50 levels. The lab is added to the workshop totals.
The Defense Percent cap of 98% is reachable without going beyond level 23 of this lab (+4.60%) with Workshop upgrades (+49.50%), the Extra Defense card (+11%), Perks (+25%), and Sub-Module effects (+8%).
