Multishot Chance
Chance to shoot more than one projectile when firing. There are 99 levels in the workshop. Each level raises the chance by 0.50% for a maximum of 49.50% chance to activate.
Multishot Targets
Number of projectiles shot when multishot activates if enough enemies are in range. There are 7 levels. The starting value is 2 targets and increases by 1 target each level for a maximum of 9 targets.
