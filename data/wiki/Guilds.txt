Unlocking Guilds
Guilds are unlocked with Milestone Tier 3 Wave 10, once unlocked a player can join an existing guild or create their own. A total of 30 players may be in any one guild.
Creating a Guild
A user once in the guild screen can create their own Guild for 50
Gems
, in doing so they will select their own Name, Description and Icon.  In addition they are able to set the joining criteria for the guild,
Free ( No Approval required), With Approval (Guild Leader to approve each joiner), Invitation Only (The guild will not appear on the available guild list).
Joining a Guild
A Player can search the available guild list and request to join the shown guilds.  Depending on the admission requirements of the guild the player may automatically join the guild.
Guild Rewards
Joining a guild will give you access to your
Guardian
.  The players contribution to the guild is earnt from
Weekly Challenge
boxes. The coin reward is based off of highest unlocked tier. A player can find their base coin reward by looking at
Daily Missions
Unspent Guild
Tokens
will be auto cashed out at the end of each Guild
Season
(8 weeks), with any unspent tokens will be converted into gems at a 5:1 ratio, rounded up (5 Guild Tokens = 1 Gem).

Themes
Themes
will rotate each season. Themes are available for a limited time and can be missed. Themes have returned in the past but there is no certainty a theme will return in the future. Once purchased, theme will be available for use forever. There will be 1 tower theme available for 100 tokens, 1 background for 100 tokens, 1 Menu for 100 Tokens and 1 Guardian Skin for 150 tokens.
Relics
The relics are at the price-points of 75 and 150 tokens. The relics are also designated as
'limited,'
so they may be unique to the event that sponsors them.
