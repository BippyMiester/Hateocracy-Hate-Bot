Overview
Chance to skip enemy stat level up after completing a wave.
Workshop
Unlock in
workshop
for 1,000,000,000 (= 1B) coins after
Recovery Packages
. Unlocks Enemy Attack Level Skip and Enemy Health Level Skip. Both workshops have 699 levels with a base of 0.05% chance to skip enemy levels. Each level increases the chance by 0.05% for a maximum of 35.00%.
When skipping a wave with the card Wave Skip, Enemy Level Skips will be checked for each skipped wave. Ex: if wave skips activates x3, Enemy level skip can be triggered multiple times based on the Enemy Level Skip percents, deterministically. [As of V26] Both Enemy Attack Level Skip and Enemy Health Level Skip are completely non random. For example: A 50% chance will skip every other wave with no variance.
