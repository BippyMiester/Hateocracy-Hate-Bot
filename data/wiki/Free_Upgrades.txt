Overview
Free Upgrades are separated by each workshop: Attack, Defense and Utility. They provide a chance for their corresponding upgrade to level up for free during rounds after each wave. If the
Wave Skip
card activates, Free Upgrades can trigger an additional time per workshop for each wave skipped. If Free-Upgrades chance is over 100%, there is a chance to get another upgrade. For example, If the Free Utility Upgrade has a chance of 110%, you would have a 90% chance of receiving one upgrade, and a 10% chance of receiving 2 for that wave.

workshop
for 800 coins after
Coins/Kill Bonus
and
Coins/Wave
