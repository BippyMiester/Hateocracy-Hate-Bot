Recharge Demon Mode
Description
Once per round, activate Demon mode, increasing the damage by x3 and becoming invincible for 180/200/220/240/260/280/300 sec.
Use
The Demon Mode
card
requires manual activation. Optimal use involves activating it when near death to extend the length of your run.
With
Smart Demon Mode Automation
, it is one of several abilities that prevent death, activating in the following order where possible:
Death Defy
: If lethal damage would be taken, Death Defy may activate instead.
Energy Shield
: If
any
damage would be taken and Death Defy did not activate, Energy Shield will expend a charge instead.
Second Wind
: If lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Second Wind will activate instead.
Demon Mode: With The Vault|Smart Demon Mode Automation, if lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Demon Mode will activate instead.
Nuke
: With
Smart Nuke Automation
, if lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Nuke will activate instead. This does not destroy in-flight projectiles, but as of
v27.3 (18 Dec, 2025)
Smart Nuke Automation also provides 15 seconds of invulnerability.
