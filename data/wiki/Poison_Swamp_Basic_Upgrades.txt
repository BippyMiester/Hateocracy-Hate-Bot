Basic Upgrades
Basic upgrades for all Ultimate Weapons are located in the
Ultimate Weapon
menu after unlocking the Ultimate Weapon. Unlocking grants the abilities to upgrade Damage, Duration and Cooldown using stones. Enemies receive damage from 1 poison swamp when entering overlapping poison swamps.
Costs
Damage has 30 levels, Duration has 14 levels and Cooldown has 15 levels. The total stone cost is 19196.
