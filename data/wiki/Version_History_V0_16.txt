v0.16
Leaderboards
• Leaderboards for highest wave and tier now available. Higher tier always takes precedence over a higher wave on lower tier. It's a start, will evaluate what changes need to be made as it collects data and such. Maybe make is seasonal or add new rankings later
Workshop Upgrades
• A large number of upgrades had their max level increased and pricing for these new levels adjusted
• A large number of upgrade values also received exponential scaling at particular levels hit (example is damage now receives extra exponential damage increases at level 1500, 2000, 2500, etc)
• Attack damage equation adjusted for level 1000 and above to increase faster exponentially
• Attack max level increased from 5000 to 6000
• Crit Factor max level increased from 99 to 150
• Dmg/Meter increase per level decay reduced, ends at level 70 and from there is +.20% per level now (buff)
• Dmg/Meter max level increased from 99 to 200
• Bounce Shot Range max level increased from 40 to 60
• Super Crit Mult max level increased from 80 to 120
• Defense absolute scaling increased from level 300 and above
• Health Regen scaling from level 400 and above increased
Cards
• New Epic Card: Wave Accelerator
• New Epic Card: Berserker (balance of this card very likely to change after feedback)
• New Common Card: Fortress
• All cards now have a 7th level added to them
• 1 new unlockable card slot
• Health regen card buffed: 1.20 base to 1.40, increase per level from .10 to .20
• Inner Orbs distance from the tower is now absolute and not based on tower range
• Super Tower base bonus increased from 2.0 to 2.5, increase per level from .3 to .4
• Card panel re-sized to better show more cards
Ultimate Weapons
• Death wave quantity max level raised from 3 to 5
• Chain lightning damage per level exponential factor increased
• Smart missile damage per level exponential factor greatly increased
• Inner landmine damage per level exponential factor slightly increased
• Poison swamp damage per level exponential factor slightly increased
Tournaments
• Difficulty scaling for tournaments increased again to make rounds shorter (enemy health, attack, speed exponential growth factor)
Music
• One 3 new tracks by Krisu. One is unlocked instantly, 2 others available to unlock in the events shop with medals
• Music Manager Panel within themes panel available for players that unlock any theme or track once. Can toggle songs on/off, pick preference of loop or play next track in active rounds
• On game start, it shuffles track choice
Orbs
• Inner Orbs card renamed to Extra Orbs
• Color of extra orbs changed to purple
• Distance for extra orbs changed. Minimum is now a range of 6.0 from tower and max of tower range x 1.3 (performance no longer tied to tower range)
• Normal orbs distance from tower increased slightly
Other
• Projectile speed factor increased based on tower attack speed
• Upgrade buttons that can't be afforded have their text darkened
• Enemy mass raised slightly
• Enemy speed increases for wave 100 and above slowly scales up more for shorter slightly shorter rounds
• Panel for events tells player to update if needed for current event (instead of just not loading)
• Current and max level shown in upgrade detail panels
• New panel under upgrade detail showing other details such as health gained from death wave and health from berserker card (in active rounds)
• Scroll sensitivity reduced on in game upgrade panels (easier to tap buttons)
• Health regen perk benefit increased from .60 to .75 per level
• Animations for some buttons
Bugs Fixed
• Encyclopedia attack panel upgrades out of order
• Couple odd animations playing on first start of the game
• Lab upgrades for interest showing for players without interest unlocked in workshop
• When unlocking an ultimate weapon perk, remember the state (on/off) you left it in if you own that weapon
• Coins not updating when opening event panel from in round
• Tower explosion on death no showing when themes are active
• Translation fixes
