Overview
The wall is a defensive ability that blocks enemies from approaching your tower until its health has hit 0. Once it hits zero, it will rebuild over time. It does not stop enemies while rebuilding
Workshop
Unlocked in
workshop
for 500 million coins after
Death Defy
. Unlocks Wall Health and Wall Rebuild.
Wall Health
Maximum health of the wall is a percentage of tower
health
. Has 1800 upgrades with a starting percentage of 20% and increases by 0.1% per level for a maximum of 200.00% tower health.
Wall Rebuild
Time it takes to rebuild the wall after it is destroyed. Wall Rebuild will push all enemies out from the tower to ensure no enemies are trapped inside. Has 300 upgrades each reducing the rebuild time by 2 seconds for a minimum of 600 seconds. The hard cap on wall rebuild currently is 150s.

Lab
Unlock labs in
Milestones
. Wall Health and Wall Rebuild are unlocked in tier 8 wave 10. Wall health adds to the workshop value and wall rebuild reduces the workshop value. Wall Regen and Wall Thorns are unlocked in tier 10 wave 70. Wall Regen applies a percentage of tower
regen
to the wall. Wall Thorns allows the wall to deal a percentage of tower
thorns
to the enemies when attacked. Wall Invincibility is unlocked on tier 12 wave 10. Wall Invincibility makes the wall invincible after being rebuilt for a period of time. Wall Fortification is unlocked on tier 14 wave 60. Wall Fortification allows the wall to regenerate beyond its maximum health. It requires Wall Regen to be able to heal health beyond maximum.
