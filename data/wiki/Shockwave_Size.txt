Overview
A wave that spawns periodically that causes enemies to be moved away from the tower.
Workshop
Unlocked in
Workshop
for 100,000 coins after
Orbs
. Unlocks Shockwave Size and Shockwave Frequency.
Shockwave Size
Size of shockwave to push enemies away (except for bosses). There are 35 levels in the workshop with a base value of 0.6. Each level increases the size by 0.05 for a maximum value of 2.35.
Shockwave Frequency
There are 40 levels with a base value of 20 seconds. Each level decreases the frequency by 0.15 seconds for a minimum frequency of 14.00 seconds. The hard cap for shockwave frequency is 7 seconds.
