Acquiring Elite Cells
Elite Cells can only be obtained by defeating Elite enemies: Vampire, Ray and Scatter.
For Tiers 1 through 13, each Elite drops between 1 and X cells, where X is the current Tier. Average drop rate is (1+X)/2 cells per elite.

These are known ways to improve your elite cells:
1. Upgrading

(Lab)

3. Farm at higher waves.
4. Cells Bonus in Workshop Enhancements

Cells/Kill Bonus
Requires 500 billion coins to be spent on Utility enhancements to unlock. Increases Cells/Kill by x1 when unlocked and increases by 0.01x per level. Has a maximum level of 200 for a 3x bonus.
