Description
Chance of a killing hit to the tower being ignored.
Activation Priority
Death Defy is one of several abilities that prevent death, activating in the following order where possible:
Death Defy: If lethal damage would be taken, Death Defy may activate instead.
Energy Shield
: If
any
damage would be taken and Death Defy did not activate, Energy Shield will expend a charge instead.
Second Wind
: If lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Second Wind will activate instead.
Demon Mode
: With
Smart Demon Mode Automation
, if lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Demon Mode will activate instead.
Nuke
: With
Smart Nuke Automation
, if lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Nuke will activate instead. This does not destroy in-flight projectiles, but as of
v27.3 (18 Dec, 2025)
Smart Nuke Automation also provides 15 seconds of invulnerability.
Workshop
Unlocked in the
workshop
for 1,500,000 coins after
Land Mine Chance, Damage and Radius
. There are 75 upgrades in the workshop for 0.4% per upgrade for a total of 30.00% chance to Death Defy.
