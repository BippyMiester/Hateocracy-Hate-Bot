Example of submodule rarity.
Sub-Module Effects
are available on all Modules. Rarity of the sub-module is independent of the rarity of the module itself.  Notice in the picture to the right that this is an Epic module with a Rare and a Common sub-module. These sub-modules can be rerolled using reroll shards. Existing Sub-modules can be locked, so their slot doesn't get rerolled, but this increases the cost. See
Modules
for more information on submodules.
Note that additional stats from Assist Module Sub-Module effects does not bypass hard caps on stats, such as limit of 98% defense, 90% slow for Chrono Field Speed reduction, 150s Wall rebuild, 7s Shockwave Frequency, 40% Death Defy, and Inner Land Mine CD of 37s.
