Overview
Criticals provide a chance to deal bonus damage to enemies.
Workshop
Unlocked in
Workshop
for free. Critical Chance and Critical Factor are unlocked at the start of the game.
Critical Chance
Chance of each projectile to deal critical damage. Critical Chance has 79 levels in the workshop. Each level raises the chance by 1% for a maximum of 80% chance from the workshop.
