Gems
Gems are a core currency to buy special items and upgrades with.
Acquiring Gems
Players can acquire Gems in many different a few ways:
Claiming the Floating Gems that spawns around your tower. Also known as Bob.
A maximum of 10 floating Gems can be spawned in a run.
You gain two Gems per floating Gem.
Floating Gems spawn about once every 400-500 waves, and no more than once every 15 minutes.
Claiming the Ad Gems (Appears approximately every 10 minutes on your screen)
Five Gems per claim.
Completing Offer walls/TapJoy.
Claiming the Free Daily Gems in the store.
Participating in Tournaments.
Purchasing Gems with real money.
