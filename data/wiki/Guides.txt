Guides are player created. The guides found here are to help give guidance based on what has been found to work well. These guides are used to optimize your gameplay. When reading a guide, always check for the last version it was updated for. Using old guides and advice can lead to undesired results. It is possible to progress without following the guides.
The most important thing is to play in a way that is fun for you!
