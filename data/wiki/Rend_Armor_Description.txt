Description
Rend armor gives the chance to apply a damage multiplier. The multiplier is applied to single enemies and increases tower projectile damage only.
Workshop
Unlocked in the
workshop
for 500,000,000,000 (500 Billion) coins after
Super Critical
. Unlocks Rend Armor Chance and Rend Armor Mult. Rend Armor provides a multiplier on effected enemies up to 800% of normal damage.
Rend Armor Chance
Chance on enemy hit to permanently increase the damage that enemy takes from projectiles. There are 299 levels with each level increasing the chance by 0.10%. The starting chance is 0.10% and has a maximum of 30.00%.
Rend Armor Mult
Amount of additional damage that the enemy takes. Rend can stack to a limit of 800.00%. There are 299 levels with each increasing the multiplier by 0.10% or 0.0010X. The starting value is 0.0010X and has a maximum value of 0.3000X. The multiplier only applies to tower projectiles. Damage from other sources are not modified by Rend Armor.
