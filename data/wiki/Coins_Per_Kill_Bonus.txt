Description
Coins earned from destroying enemies.
Workshop
Unlocked in
workshop
for 100 coins with
Coins/Wave
after
Cash Bonus
and
Cash Per Wave
. There are 149 levels with a base value of x1.00. Each level increases the base by 0.01 for a maximum value of x2.49.
Coins/Kill is multiplicative with all other bonuses that increase coins earned from kills. Example of bonuses include: Tier Bonus,
Skin
Bonus,
Golden Bot
Golden Tower
. These do not change the display of coin/kill. The All Coin
perk
will increase the displayed Coin/Kill value.
Formula
Coins/Kill (CPK) = (Workshop × CPK lab + CPK mod effect) × (1 + coin bonus enhancement) × (1 + coin bonus perk base [0.15] × perk quantity) × (trade-off perk [1.8] × (1 + trade-off lab))
Solve the formula without rounding. Rounding will change the result from the display in game.
Enhancement is double counted in both the tier bonus and in cpk.
If no trade-off unlocked, replace with 1.
