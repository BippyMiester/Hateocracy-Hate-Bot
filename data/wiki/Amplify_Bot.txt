Description
Amplify damage against enemies inside range.
Unlocking/Upgrading
Like all bots, Amplify Bot is unlocked and upgraded in the
Event Shop
menu. The unlock cost will depend on how many other bots have been bought. Each of the four amplify bot upgrades start with a base upgrade cost of 100 medals and increase by 40 medals each level.
Increases the amount of time Amplify Bot is active. Duration starts at 20 seconds and has 30 levels.  Each level increases duration by 0.5 seconds.
Cooldown
The amount of time between initial activations. Cooldown starts at 120 seconds and has 15 levels.  Each level decreases cooldown by 3 seconds.
Bonus
Increases the damage bonus against enemies. Bonus starts at x3.50 and has 30 levels.  Each level increases bonus by 0.4.
Range
Increases the range the bot covers when active. Range is amplified by tower range. Range starts at 25m and has 15 levels.  Each level increases range by 2m.
