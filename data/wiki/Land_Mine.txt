Overview
A defense workshop that has a chance to spawn, dealing damage to all enemies in range when triggered.
Land Mine Chance
Chance of enemies dropping a land mine.
Workshop
Unlocked in
workshop
for 400,000 coins with Land Mine Damage and Land Mine Radius after
Shockwave Size
and
Shockwave Frequency
. There are 50 upgrades that increase the chance by 0.60% for each upgrade for a maximum of 30.00% chance to spawn. Once spawned, the land mine will explode after a set time that can be increased with the Land Mine Decay lab.
Land Mine Damage
Percent of tower damage dealt by land mines (Accounts for crit/super crit factor and chance).
Workshop
Unlocked in workshop for 400,000 coins with Land Mine Chance and Land Mine Radius after Shockwave Size and Shockwave Frequency. There are 200 upgrades with a base value of 100%. Each level increases damage by 10% for a maximum of 2100% damage.
Card
The Land Mine Stun
card
gives land mines the ability to stun non-boss enemies in land mine range when they explode.
Damage Calculation
Land mine damage is using an average crit factor and average super crit factor based on the activation chances. The damage can be calculated with the following formula:
Land Mine Damage = Land Mine Damage % *
Damage
* (1 +
Critical Factor
Critical Chance
) * (1 +
Super Crit Mult
Super Crit Chance
* Crit Chance)
Land Mine Radius
Explosion radius of land mines
Workshop
Unlocked in workshop for 400,000 coins with Land Mine Chance and Land Mine Damage after Shockwave Size and Shockwave Frequency. There are 50 upgrades for a total of 1.50.
