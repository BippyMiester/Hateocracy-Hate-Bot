Tournaments
Every tournament for silver tier and above will challenge players by applying 2 battle conditions
The battle conditions scale more aggressively in higher tier tournaments
These conditions will increase in difficulty as you reach higher waves
Ultimate Weapons PLUS
Every ultimate weapon has received a PLUS upgrade
PLUS upgrades add an additional unique ability to each Ultimate Weapon
PLUS upgrades become unlockable once every ultimate weapon is unlocked
Gameplay
Ultimate Weapon timers will stay synchronized with each other now, instead of diverging slightly as the round progresses
Cards are locked when bosses are active
Fix tower range indicator to more accurately represent range
Added 6 new relics for upcoming events
Limited ultimate weapon toggling to 40 times per round
