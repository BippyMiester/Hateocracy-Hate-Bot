Ultimate Weapon Plus - Golden Combo
Ultimate Weapon Plus abilities can only be unlocked when all 9 Ultimate Weapons are unlocked. Golden Combo increases cash and coins earned after Golden Tower finishes by x% for each kill. The equation to determine the bonus is shown below:
Bonus multiplier = ((1 + 0.0003 x (Level + 1))^Kill) - 1
Golden Combo will only count killed enemies that spawned during the activation. Enemies that were alive before the current Golden tower activation will not be included in the kill count.

Final bonus
The final bonus received very much depends on the duration of the GT.
K = Kills during GT = Kills per second * duration = Kps * d
B = Bonus multiplier = ((1 + 0.0003 x (Level + 1))^K) - 1
I = Income during GT = K * income per mob
T = Number of GT activations = Total duration / GT CD
FI = Final income = T * (I + I * B) => T * I * (1 + B) => T * Kps * d * income per mob * ((1 + 0.0003 x (Level + 1))^(Kps * d))
We can solve for FI(d+1) vs FI(d) as
T * Kps * (d + 1) * income per mob * ((1 + 0.0003 x (Level + 1))^(Kps * (d+1)))
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
T * Kps * d * income per mob * ((1 + 0.0003 x (Level + 1))^(Kps * d))
Which reduces to
(d + 1)
------- * ((1 + 0.0003 x (Level + 1))^Kps)
