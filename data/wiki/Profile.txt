Description
The player profile is a menu that allows players to display their achievements to other players during tournaments.
Accessing
To access the player profile, click on the tower on the home menu
Display
The profile allows players to select a
tower skin
along with 5
relics
to display. These selections are cosmetic only and do not make relics or skins active.
The profile menu also displays your highest
tournament
ranking and highest tier and wave. You may change your tournament name in this menu as well.
Profiles now include the guild you are currently active in. (this was added in v26.0)
