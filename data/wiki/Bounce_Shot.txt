Description
Gives the tower a chance for their projectiles to bounce after hitting an enemy a certain number of times when enemies are in range.
Workshop
Unlocked in
workshop
for 10000 coins after unlocking
Rapid Fire
. Unlocks Bounce Shot Chance, Bounce Shot Targets and Bounce Shot Range.
Bounce Shot Chance
Chance for a projectile to bounce to another enemy after hitting. There are 85 levels in the workshop. Each level increases the chance by 0.80% for a maximum chance of 68.00%.
Bounce Shot Targets
Number of times a projectile can bounce from enemy to enemy. There are 7 levels in the workshop with each level adding 1 target. The base number of targets is 1 and has a maximum of 8 targets. The maximum targets can be increased to 14 using the Bounce Shot
perk
. Enemies targeted by bounce shot cannot be hit toward the tower with
knockback
Bounce Shot Range
Range that a projectile can bounce from enemy to enemy if activated. There are 60 levels with each level increasing the range by 0.1m. The starting value is 2.0m and has a maximum distance of 8.0m. Once a target has been selected by bounce shot, the projectile will chase that enemy even if it has been knocked farther than the maximum bounce range.
