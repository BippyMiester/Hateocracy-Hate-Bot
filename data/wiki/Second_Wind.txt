Description
Revive the tower with half health once per round and creates an invincible shield for 10/15/20/25/30/35/40 sec.
Use
The Second Wind
card
is one of several abilities that prevent death, activating in the following order where possible:
Death Defy
: If lethal damage would be taken, Death Defy may activate instead.
Energy Shield
: If
any
damage would be taken and Death Defy did not activate, Energy Shield will expend a charge instead.
Second Wind: If lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Second Wind will activate instead.
Demon Mode
: With
Smart Demon Mode Automation
, if lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Demon Mode will activate instead.
Nuke
: With
Smart Nuke Automation
, if lethal damage would be taken and no higher-priority death prevention is available, Nuke will activate instead. This does not destroy in-flight projectiles, but as of
v27.3 (18 Dec, 2025)
Smart Nuke Automation also provides 15 seconds of invulnerability.
