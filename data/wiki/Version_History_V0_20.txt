v0.20
New Tiers
T14 and T15 have been added, introducing new challenges and opportunities for advancement
Battle Conditions
Battle conditions modify typical battles with special rules, often increasing the difficulty
Expect to encounter them in T14 and T15, adding new twists to the gameplay in these tiers
Relics
Relics are collectibles that permanently improve your tower with stacking effects.
Discover them by achieving difficult tasks and participating in  events
Player Profile
Show your progress to other players
Choose a tower skin as your avatar, which will be displayed in tournaments and rankings
Lightspeed Shots
Research the lab to enable your tower to shoot at lightspeed, eliminating the travel time of shots to the enemy
Milestones
A unique 4,500 milestone reward has been added to each tier
Milestone Premium Pass 5 added
New Card: Ultimate Crit
Introducing the Ultimate Crit card
Grants your ultimate weapons a chance to critically hit using the Tower Critical Factor Workshop multiplier
Range Controls
Players can now adjust their Range Lab research to any level. Visit the Range lab to adjust the level
Poison Swamp Visual Overhaul
The Poison Swamp ultimate weapon has received a visual overhaul to better match the game style
Bug Fixes and Details
iPad optimized for better menu and visuals
Fixed Golden Tower position not centered on tower
Fixed a bug that could cause you to start the game with a trade-off perk
Resolved an issue with Cloud save when changing devices on iOS, where it could load an older save
Fixed the Force Cloud Save function not updating the last save time correctly
Resolved issues with all Ultimate Weapons where sometimes smart missiles or inner land mines would keep growing on screen
Resuming a round will no longer remove the number of protectors you had on the screen
Fixed Multishot not selecting targets correctly, sometimes shooting at the same target simultaneously
The mail panel has been enlarged with a bigger font.
Fixed a bug that could display more than one tournament daily mission
Translations updated and Arabic added as a new language
