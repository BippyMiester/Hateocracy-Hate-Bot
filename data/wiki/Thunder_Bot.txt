Description
Stuns enemies in an area around the bot. Once it ends, enemies will be slowed down by the linger effect. Stun and linger are 50% effective against Bosses and Elites.
Unlocking/Upgrading
Like all bots, Thunder Bot is unlocked and upgraded in the
Event Shop
menu. The unlock cost will depend on how many other bots have been bought. Each of the 3 Thunder bot upgrades start with a base upgrade cost of 100 medals and increase by 40 medals each level.
Increase the duration enemies are stunned. Has 20 levels.
Cooldown
The amount of time between initial activations. Cooldown starts at 120 seconds and has 15 levels.
Linger
Increases the slow percentage of the linger effect. Starts at 20% and has 20 levels.
Range
Increases the range the bot covers when active. Range is amplified by tower range. Has 15 levels.
