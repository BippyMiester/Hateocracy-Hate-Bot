card
increases the health regen workshop when equipped. The card multiplies with the lab.
There are 3 ways to multiply Health Regen with cards:
Health Regen Card (common): x2.6
Health Regen Card (mastery): x5
Second Wind (mastery): x10 for 400 waves
Workshop Enhancement
Health Regen
Requires 50 billion coins to be spent on defense enhancements to unlock. Increases the health regen multiplier by x1 when unlocked and increases by 0.01x per level. Has a maximum level of 400 for a 5x bonus.
