Description
The Critical Workshop provides bonus damage to tower projectiles. The critical workshops are used in calculating Ultimate Weapon and Land Mine damage.
Workshop
Unlocked in
Workshop
for free. Critical Chance and Critical Factor are unlocked at the start of the game.
Critical Chance
Chance of each projectile to deal critical damage. Critical Chance has 79 levels in the workshop. Each level raises the chance by 1% for a maximum of 80% chance from the workshop. Critical Chance can be increased with the Critical Chance Card and Critical Chance
Cannon Module Effect
Cards
The Critical Chance
card
raises the chance up to 11%.
Critical Factor
Damage multiplier for critical hits. Critical Factor is used to calculate Ultimate Weapon critical hits when using the Ultimate Crit
card
. Has 150 levels with a base multiplier of 1.2 that increase the multiplier by 0.1 each level for a maximum of 16.2. Critical Factor max can be increased using the Critical Factor Lab, Critical Factor Cannon Module Effect and Critical Factor Enhancement.
