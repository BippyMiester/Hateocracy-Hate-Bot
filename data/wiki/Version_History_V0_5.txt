vO.5.0
Features
Translated into 8 new languages - check settings panel to switch to your native language
New Card (common) - Enemy Balance
New Card (rare) - Critical Coin
New Card (epic) - Super Tower
New Card (epic) - Second Wind
New Defense Upgrades - Land Mines
New Defense Upgrade - Death Defy
Balance
Enemy base attack damage for higher tiers decreased, decreased more for highest tiers
Smart Missiles: Cost of upgrading number of missiles reduced a lot
Smart Missiles: Max missiles increased from 10 to 14
Golden tower base cool down increased from 180 to 270 seconds, max level increased from 12 to 15
Chain lightning base chance reduced from 10 to 5%, increase per level decreased from 3.0 to 1.5%
Chain lightning Base damage increased from 40 to 70%, damage per level increased from 5 to 10%
Ultimate weapon Land Mines renamed to Inner Land Mines
Inner land mines explosion radius increased from .75 to .90
Inner land mines base damage increased from 600 to 800%, increase per level from 40 to 50%
Inner land mine contact size increased slightly
Death Wave base damage increased from 120 to 150%, upgrade per level from 10 to 15%
Lifesteal increase per upgrade decreased from .15 to .14%
Shockwave size increase per upgrade increased from .04 to .05
Bugs
Projectiles sometimes affecting the incorrect enemies when a block intercepts a shot
Total coins on Battle panel not fitting in text box
Notification for lab research completing firing by mistake
Many other small bugs
