Power Stones
Power Stones are a premium currency that can be used to buy and upgrade
Ultimate Weapons
. Purchases that are
{\displaystyle \geq}
500 Power Stones will have a purchase confirmation message.
Acquiring Power Stones
Players can acquire Power Stones by:
Participating in
Tournaments
Exchanging
Medals
Weekly rewards from
Daily Missions
Milestones
Purchasing Monthly Stone Packs and/or other promotional offers via:
The Tower Web Store (online)
Google Play Store (in-game)
Apple App Store (in-game)
