Summon Chip
The Summon Chip spawns 2 enemies per second while active, of type Basic, Tank, Ranged, and Fast. Additionally, the Summon Chip gives a cash bonus to all killed enemies while active, not just those summoned. The summoned enemies will not gain Ultimate Status, even if the
Battle Condition
of their type is active.
The chip launched in Season 5, with an incorrect description, causing confusion. This was quickly corrected on The Tower
Discord
server.
