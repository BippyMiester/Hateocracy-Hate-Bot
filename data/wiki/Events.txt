Description
Events are a series of challenges that reward medals when completed. Unlocked in
Milestones
on tier 1 wave 70.
Event Cycle
Events occur on a 2 week cycle. They last for 2 weeks with the first 8 days giving new missions and 6 days without any new missions. When the running event ends, the next one will immediately begin.
Missions
All event missions have  3 tiers to complete. Each tier will have a higher requirement and provide an increasing amount of medals.
There are 7 missions unlocked on the first day with 2 missions added everyday for the next 7  days. Missions will remain until the end of the event.

Rewards

Event Shop
Event shop is where medals can be spent. The shop will remain open even when there is no active event. The shop offers currencies,
themes
, relics, songs and bots.
Currencies
Gems, stones and shards are available to be purchased each event. The cost increases each purchase and will reset at the beginning of the next event. Exchanging medals will give 150 gems, 15 stones or 250 shards.
There are 4 types of shards available for purchase corresponding with each
Module
type. Each purchase of shards will increase the cost of the other types.

Themes
Themes
will rotate each event. Themes are available for a limited time and can be missed. Themes have returned in the past but there is no certainty a theme will return in the future. Once purchased, theme will be available for use forever. There will be 1 tower theme available for 150 medals and 1 background for 250 medals.

The relics are at the price-points of 200 and 500 medals. The relics are also designated as
'limited,'
so they may be unique to the event that sponsors them.
Relics are also earned for completing events. Earning 350 medals unlocks the first-tier Relic. Earning 700 medals unlocks the second (and final) tier Relic. Purchasing the event pass unlocks access to 2 extra relics, one at 550 medals and another at 1100 medals.
Songs
There are 3 songs available. These songs do not cycle and cost 200 medals each. Each one provides a +0.6% coin bonus.
Bots

Bot Respec
Bots and their spent medals can be refunded through a bot respec. Bot respec is available for 300 Gems once per event. Labs completed for bots are not refunded through a bot respec, only medal related upgrades/unlocks.
