v0.22 - The Modules Update
Modules
Unlock the modules in Milestones (Tier 2, Wave 90) and start making your Tower even stronger!
You will be able to equip modules of 4 different categories to enhance your tower capabilities. Each category has a main stat and some random sub stats
Epic Modules also have a Unique Effect that you can't find anywhere else
Module Types:
Cannon
Main stat - x% Tower Damage
Sub-Effects - Attack Upgrades
Armor
Main stat - x% Tower Health
Effects - Defense Upgrades
Generator
Main stat » x% Coin Bonus
Effects » Utility Upgrades
Core
Main stat » x% Ultimate Weapon Damage
Effects » Ultimate Weapons Upgrades
Bug Fixes and balance changes
Fixed performance issues that was being caused by bounce shot
Increased limit of Def% from 90% to 98%
Fixed Ranged attack not charging Berserk
