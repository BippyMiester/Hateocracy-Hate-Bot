Description
Labs Speed decreases the amount of time needed to complete
lab research
Increasing the level of this research
does not
reduce the amount of time to research subsequent levels of this research, but earning
relics
that increase lab speed
does
reduce how long it takes to complete each level of this research.
For research other than Lab Speed research, such as
Game Speed
or
Standard Perks Bonus
research, the lab speed bonus from this research stacks
multiplicatively
with the bonus to lab speed that you receive from relics. For example, if you receive a x1.25 lab speed bonus from your relics and a x2.98 lab speed bonus from your Lab Speed research, your total lab speed bonus will be x3.725, not x3.23. The equation is (Lab Speed Bonus) = [(Bonus from Lab Speed Research) x (Bonus from Relics)]. Thus, it is highly recommended to heavily prioritize obtaining relics that increase your bonus to lab speed if they appear during an event.
