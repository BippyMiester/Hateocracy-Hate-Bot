Unmerge Module
Unlock Lab in Milestones at tier 8 wave 20. Allows modules to be unmerged. Modules that required a duplicate epic+ to merge will provide the lower level module and the epic+. When any module could be used to merge to the new rarity, unmerging will convert the modules into shards of the unmerged type. Has 1 level.
